Mikron

The best mini player for Apple Music.

Overview

Experience a music player designed completely around your content. Introducing Mikron, the cleanest, most elegant mini player for Apple Music.


Mikron elevates the functionality you love from existing players to new levels. You no longer have to settle for a microscopic window or full-screen view just to see the title of that cool song you've never heard before, because Mikron is resizable to your liking – without having to give up important information.


Mikron keeps the focus on your music, and modifies its design accordingly. Your song's album art is the centerpiece of the app, with a background that changes to match its colour scheme so your tunes never look out of place.


Unlike others, this player is not cluttered with annoying controls that you'll never use. Rather, its interface has been streamlined to solely present rewind, play/pause, fast-forward, and time controls. This way, random elements will never interfere with your favourite songs.


Requires Apple Music for macOS Big Sur 11.0 or later.

Why should I use a mini player?

Mini players like Mikron are a great way to keep track of the music you're listening to without having to pull up a large media window that wastes space while you're working.


You can also use them as a full-screen window to elegantly present the currently playing song at a gathering, while you're away from your computer, or simply to fill up a secondary display.
