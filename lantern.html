Quick Tips

Welcome to Lantern! Get started with the tips below.

Getting Started

Launch Lantern; you can see if it's active based on whether its icon is present in the menu bar. Then, just hit Option+Space on your keyboard simultaneously to perform a search or enter a URL.

Tips

Open at Login: You can choose to have Lantern open automatically at login, so that it's always available when you hit Option+Space. Launch the app and click its menu bar icon to toggle.

URL Support: You can paste URLs straight into the Lantern search bar, and Lantern will open it directly for you when you press Enter. You can also click the magnifying glass icon to toggle between Search and URL modes.

Keyboard Controls: You can navigate results with the arrow keys, so you don't need to grab your mouse.

Copy Results: Right-click results to bring up a context menu. From there, you can click Copy.
