Quick Tips

Welcome to Mikron! Get started with the tips below.

Getting Started

Launch Mikron and Apple Music on your Mac, pick a song, and watch Mikron come to life. It's that simple.

Controls

Mikron features four controls for quick interaction with Apple Music.

Play/Pause

Select this button to play or pause your music.

Previous Track

Select this button to return to the previous track.

Next Track

Select this button to jump to the next track.

Scrubber

Move this slider to change the elapsed time of your music.

Settings

You can modify Mikron's behaviour to better suit you and your desktop.


Size of the player.

Select between the following size preferences to change the size of Mikron's content, and thus how small the window can be shrunken to:

  • Standard: The default size of Mikron.
  • Small: This size decreases the font and button sizes of all elements, and shrinks the maximum height of the album art.
  • Micro: This size retains the font and button sizes of the Small option, but also removes the album art.

Use blurred album art as background?

Decide whether you would like a blurred version of your song's album art to be used as the app's background. This is on by default.


Float Mikron above other windows?

Decide whether you would like Mikron to float above the rest of the windows on your desktop, regardless of which one you're focused on.
